Colonial Charm of Oaxaca

Cultural Escapes

Experience the enchanting ambiance of Oaxaca's colonial heritage as you stroll through cobblestone streets lined with colorful buildings and vibrant markets. Delight in the aromas of traditional cuisine and marvel at the intricate craftsmanship of local artisans.

  • Duration: 4 days

  • Highlights: Explore UNESCO-listed historic sites, visit artisan workshops, indulge in Oaxacan cuisine, witness traditional dances.

  • Accommodation: Boutique hotels in the heart of Oaxaca's historic district.

  • Price: Starting from $800 per person

Mayan Mysteries in Yucatan

Cultural Escapes

Uncover the secrets of the ancient Mayan civilization as you journey through the archaeological wonders of the Yucatan Peninsula. Discover towering pyramids, sacred temples, and mystical cenotes hidden within lush jungles.

  • Duration: 5 days

  • Highlights: Explore Mayan ruins at Chichen Itza and Uxmal, swim in sacred cenotes, learn about Mayan culture from expert guides, visit traditional Mayan villages.

  • Accommodation: Charming haciendas nestled in the Yucatan countryside.

  • Price: Starting from $1000 per person

Jungle Thrills in Chiapas

Adventure Expeditions

Embark on an adrenaline-fueled adventure through the untamed wilderness of Chiapas. Trek through dense rainforests, rappel down cascading waterfalls, and navigate through thrilling river rapids, all while surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty.

  • Duration: 6 days

  • Highlights: Hike to the summit of El Tzontehuitz volcano, explore the ancient ruins of Palenque, white-water rafting in the Lacanja River, zip-lining through the canopy.

  • Accommodation: Eco-friendly jungle lodges nestled in the heart of Chiapas.

  • Price: Starting from $1200 per person

  • Accommodation: Beachfront bungalows with panoramic ocean views.

Riviera Maya Paradise

Beach Retreats
